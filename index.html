OUR VISION

Welcome to Tonabolic!

Tonabolic isn’t just a pool of gym coaches.

Born from the vision of Ashrita Datta, Tonabolic is a collective of passionate, certified, and knowledgeable fitness professionals dedicated to helping you achieve your unique fitness goals.

By combining deep understanding of human anatomy with mindful attention to special conditions and scientifically backed training principles, our team designs personalized, result-oriented fitness programs that evolve with your progress.

Our sessions focus on specificity, progressive overload, and functional movement, creating an experience that challenges your body and sharpens your mind. We stand for innovation, collaboration, and—most importantly—keeping fitness fun and sustainable.

Headquartered in Hyderabad, Tonabolic offers personal and small-group training programs that have transformed countless lives. From one-on-one sessions to structured corporate wellness programs, we cater to diverse goals, schedules, and preferences.

We make consistency convenient by bringing fitness to your chosen space—your gym, your home, through online sessions, or even amidst the calm of outdoor parks.

Your body is built to move—to stay flexible, mobile, strong, balanced, and coordinated. It takes more than one coach to unlock all of that potential. That’s why, at Tonabolic, each client is supported by a team of 2–3 coaches, each contributing their expertise to deliver comprehensive, science-led training excellence.

At Tonabolic, we don't just train bodies; we empower transformations. With a dedicated team supporting every step of your journey, we ensure you unlock your fullest potential—building strength, flexibility, and resilience for life. Together, let’s make fitness a fulfilling, enduring part of your lifestyle.
