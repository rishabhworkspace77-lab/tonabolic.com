The Tonabolic
Franchise Model

Tonabolic follows a Franchise-Owned, Company-Operated (FOCO) structure. As a franchise partner, you focus on sales, relationships, and local growth, while the Tonabolic central team supports:

  • Coach hiring & induction
  • Weekly program design & audits
  • Ongoing trainer education
  • Operations & scheduling systems
  • Brand creatives, marketing aids & performance reviews
